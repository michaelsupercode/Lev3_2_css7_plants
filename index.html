Decorating with plants is simple

To create the most interesting display, use a mixture of plants sizes and leaf shapes. A large plant like Pippa the peace lilly, mixed with something sculptural like Susie the snake plant and a soft fern like Bertie gives an attractive range of textures. Hanging plants, like Rapunzel the golden pothos, add interest at different heights. Don't forget your pots. Matching pots will look clean and elegant, while a mix of styles and patterns looks eclectic and fun.
